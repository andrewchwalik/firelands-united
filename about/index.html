About Firelands United

Firelands United was founded in 2023 with one goal: build a club that gives local players a real pathway to keep competing at a high level while representing the Firelands region.

What started as an idea quickly became a team identity built on grit, local pride, and a commitment to doing things the right way. From Toledo to Tiffin to Lorain to Norwalk, the club brings together players and families from across Northern Ohio.

In 2025, our men's first team made a statement in the NOSL with a strong regular season finish and a growing supporter culture. In 2026, Firelands United expanded by launching a women's first team, continuing the mission to grow the game for everyone in our community.

Firelands United is more than match days. It's a community club focused on development, opportunity, and creating something lasting for the region.

What We're Building

Player Pathways

Competitive opportunities for local players to keep developing and stay in the game.

Community First

A club that reflects the Firelands region and gives supporters a team to rally behind.

Club Culture

High standards, hard work, and a team identity built around pride in the badge.
