Rosters

The Firelands United men's first team competes in the Northwest Division of the Northern Ohio Soccer League (NOSL). The roster is comprised of local players ranging from Toledo to Tiffin to Lorain to Norwalk, and everywhere in between. You can look back at all our seasons and rosters in the History tab.
