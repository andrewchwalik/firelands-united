Firelands United Press Kit

Looking for everything you need to write a story about our soccer club? You’ve found it! Here is where you’ll be able to get the specifics about Firelands United Football Club such as club statements, images, team details, and logo files.

Description

Firelands United Football Club is the first men’s and women’s amateur soccer club based in the Firelands Region of Ohio. We compete in the Northern Ohio Soccer League (NOSL), which is considered the 5th Division of the US Soccer "pyramid". Our club was founded in 2023 and plan to play our home matches in Sandusky, OH.
