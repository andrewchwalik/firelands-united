Firelands United Press Kit

Looking for everything you need to write a story about our soccer club? You’ve found it! Here is where you’ll be able to get the specifics about Firelands United Football Club such as club statements, team details, and official brand marks.

Description

Firelands United Football Club is the first men’s and women’s amateur soccer club based in the Firelands Region of Ohio. We compete in the Northern Ohio Soccer League (NOSL), which is considered the 5th Division of the US Soccer "pyramid". Our club was founded in 2023 and play our home matches in Sandusky, OH.

Mission

Our mission is simple: develop players, build community, and grow the sport. We want to provide an experience for our players that, not only improves their perfomance on the pitch, but helps them grow as individuals. Soccer is more fun with a community; our club focuses on engaging fans, community organizations, local leaders, and all of the Firelands Region to unite around the beautiful game. By combining those first two efforts, we're working toward growing the sport in our area and beyond.
