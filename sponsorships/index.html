Sponsorships

Firelands United partners with local and regional businesses to support player development, operations, and community growth across the Firelands Region. Our 2026 sponsorship options are designed to give partners meaningful visibility on matchday, across digital channels, and throughout the season.

Possibilities

The partnership possibilities are endless. Want to be the official hot dog supplier of Firelands United? Or the official home builder of the club? We can work with you to build something unique and feasible for your business.

If you have a custom idea that is not listed below, we're open to creative partnerships.
