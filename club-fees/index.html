Club Fees

The total cost for the 2026 season is based on when payment is made. Please review the fee timeline and use one of the payment options below.

Please include the player's full name in every payment note or memo so the club can confirm who has paid.

Early Window

$220

Payments made from April 1 through April 10.

Standard Window

$250

Payments made from April 11 through April 20.

Late Window

$270

Payments made after April 20.

Preferred

Zelle

Zelle is the preferred payment option because it keeps fees as low as possible for the club.

  • Zelle handle: firelandsunited
  • Include the player's full name in the memo.
  • Please double-check that you are sending to the Firelands United Zelle account before submitting payment.
Available Upon Request

ACH Transfer

ACH transfer is available for players who prefer to send payment directly from a bank account.

  • Include the player's full name with the transfer details request.
  • Email hello@firelandsunited.com to receive ACH instructions.
  • Please do not share ACH details publicly once received.
Easy Mobile Payment

Venmo

Players may send fees to Andrew’s personal Venmo account.

  • Venmo handle: @andrewchwalik
  • Include the player's full name in the payment note.
Easy Mobile Payment

Cash App

Players may also use Cash App for club fees.

  • Cash App handle: $andrewchwalik
  • Include the player's full name in the payment note.
Mail or Hand Deliver

Check

Players may also pay by check.

  • Make checks payable to: Firelands United Football Club
  • Mail to: 320 W 2nd Street, Lakeside Marblehead, OH 43440
  • Include the player's full name in the memo line.

Final Reminder

Your fee is not fully logged until the club confirms receipt and matches the payment to the player name included in the memo or note. If you are unsure whether your payment was received, contact the club before the deadline window closes.
