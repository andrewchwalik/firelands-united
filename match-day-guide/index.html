Match Day Guide

We're excited to welcome visiting teams to Firelands United. This guide covers the essentials for match day at our home field so your team knows where to go, what to send ahead of kickoff, and what to expect before and after the match.

Home Field

Tickets

General Admission

General admission is free. There is no ticket purchase required for fans attending Firelands United home matches.

Pregame

Before Kickoff

We aim to provide an organized pregame experience at Firelands United home matches. Please send the lineup, including pronunciations, to hello@firelandsunited.com at least 24 hours before kickoff. This helps with player announcements and match statistics.

Your team still needs to provide its official roster to the match day officials. Referee roster check is typically 20 minutes before kickoff, though this can vary by referee crew. Please have your starters lined up with the Firelands United starters between the two benches 7 minutes before kickoff. If a youth team is in attendance, those players will line up with the teams and be paired with senior players for the walkout.

Stream

Watch Live

We stream all home matches on our YouTube channel so supporters from anywhere can follow along. Feel free to share the stream link with your fans and on your socials.

Postgame

After the Match

After the match, please make sure no equipment or trash is left behind. We want to keep the venue clean and in good standing for future matches, and any serious damage to the facilities will be reported to league officials.

Please make sure your team has cleared the stadium 20 minutes after the final whistle. If players want to stay and talk after the match, they may do so in the parking lot.

Thank You

We Look Forward to Kickoff

Thanks for taking the time to review the match day guide. We hope it makes the away experience smoother for your team and gives everyone what they need before arriving in Sandusky.
