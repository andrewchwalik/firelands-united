Firelands United Welcomes Fisher-Titus as Main Jersey Sponsor

Firelands United is proud to announce Fisher-Titus as the club’s main jersey sponsor for the 2026 season!

As part of the partnership, the Fisher-Titus name will be featured on the front of all Firelands United jerseys across both our men’s and women’s teams. It is a major moment for the club as we continue to grow competitive soccer in the Firelands region and build a program that represents our community with pride.

Fisher-Titus has long been a trusted community asset within the Firelands region, serving families, athletes, workers, and neighbors throughout the community. Their presence is felt far beyond healthcare alone. They are part of the fabric of this area, supporting the people and communities that makes Firelands home.

For Firelands United, having Fisher-Titus on the front of our jerseys is about more than sponsorship. It is about alignment. Our club was built to create opportunity, bring people together, and give local players a team they can be proud to represent. Fisher-Titus reflects that same commitment to the region.

“We are incredibly excited to have Fisher-Titus on the front of our jerseys,” said Andrew Chwalik, Chief Soccer Officer of Firelands United. “They are one of the most important institutions in the Firelands region, and their commitment to this community is exactly the kind of partnership we want surrounding our club. Every time our men’s and women’s teams step onto the field, we’ll be proud to represent a sponsor that means so much to the people who live here.”

The partnership comes during an exciting season for Firelands United. The men’s first team continues to build on the foundation of its early years, while the women’s first team has launched its inaugural campaign. With both teams now competing under the Firelands United badge, the club is continuing to expand its reach and create new opportunities for players and supporters.

Fisher-Titus’ support helps make that growth possible.

As Firelands United continues to develop, the club remains focused on its core mission: growing the game, developing players, and building community through sport. Having a respected local partner like Fisher-Titus featured across the club’s kits is a meaningful step forward in that mission.

We are proud to welcome Fisher-Titus as our main jersey sponsor and grateful for their support of Firelands United soccer.
