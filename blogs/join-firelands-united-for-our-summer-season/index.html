Join Firelands United for Our Summer Season

January 1, 2025

Are you ready to be part of something extraordinary? Firelands United is excited to announce that we’re gearing up for our inaugural men’s soccer season this summer! As a proud member of the Northern Ohio Soccer League (NOSL) Northwest Region, we’re on the hunt for talented, passionate players to join us on this thrilling journey.

At Firelands United, we’re more than just a soccer team—we’re a community. Our mission is rooted in three core pillars: 1) Growth of the Beautiful Game: We’re dedicated to elevating soccer in our region by providing a platform for local talent to shine. 2) Player Development: Our goal is to help players reach their full potential, both on and off the field, through quality coaching and competitive play. 3)Community Through Sport: We aim to bring people together by fostering a supportive and inclusive soccer culture.

The Northern Ohio Soccer League (NOSL) is one of the premier amateur soccer leagues in the state, providing a competitive environment where teams from across the region battle it out for glory. Representing Sandusky, Ohio, Firelands United is thrilled to be part of this dynamic and growing league.

We’re searching for skilled, committed players who are eager to compete at a high level and represent Firelands United with pride. Whether you’re a seasoned athlete or a rising talent, we’d love to see what you can bring to the pitch.

What should you expect? Competitive matches against top teams in the NOSL Northwest Region. Training sessions designed to develop technical, tactical, and physical aspects of your game. An opportunity to be part of history as we launch our first-ever season. A supportive team environment that values effort, attitude, and togetherness.

Ready to lace up your boots and take the field with us? [Submit your information now](https://forms.gle/QNdvCNp1Rtmrim1KA) and become part of the Firelands United family. We'll review all applications and reach out to players who we think will be a good fit for our club.

"Firelands United is thrilled to launch our inaugural season and compete in the Northern Ohio Soccer League’s Northwest Region. This is a monumental step for our team as we strive to grow the beautiful game, foster player development, and unite our region through soccer. We’re looking for talented, dedicated players who share our passion and are ready to make history with us this summer." – Andrew Chwalik, Director of Football

This summer marks the beginning of something special for soccer in our region. Don’t miss your chance to be a part of history—join Firelands United for our inaugural season. Together, let’s build a club that will make Sandusky and the entire Firelands region proud.
