Internships

Thanks for your interest in joining Firelands United. Explore the internship tracks below, read through each role carefully, and apply for the one that best matches your strengths, interests, and goals within the club.

Event Operations Internship

Firelands United is looking for interns who want direct experience helping execute match day and club events. This role is built for someone who is organized, dependable, and comfortable working in a fast-moving environment.

You would support setup and teardown, help coordinate match-day logistics, assist with fan experience details, and keep operations running smoothly before, during, and after home matches.

  • Assist with home match setup, staffing flow, and post-match breakdown
  • Support ticketing, signage, fan zones, and general venue presentation
  • Help coordinate volunteers and day-of communication
  • Gain hands-on sports operations experience in a live club setting

Marketing Internship

This internship is for someone who wants to help grow the Firelands United brand across digital, match-day, and community channels. The role blends creative work with practical club promotion.

You may assist with social media planning, content capture, graphic coordination, sponsorship activation support, and campaign execution tied to matches, announcements, and club initiatives.

  • Support social media calendars, captions, and campaign planning
  • Assist with content collection for photos, video, and match-day storytelling
  • Help promote sponsors, club events, rosters, and news updates
  • Build real-world experience in sports marketing and club communications

Coaching Internship

The coaching internship is designed for candidates who want practical experience around training environments, player support, and team preparation. It is a strong fit for students or young coaches building toward long-term work in the game.

Interns in this role may assist with session setup, training logistics, player check-in, equipment management, and support tasks assigned by Firelands United staff throughout the season.

  • Assist coaches with training setup and equipment organization
  • Support player logistics and day-to-day team needs
  • Observe session planning, communication, and match preparation
  • Develop hands-on experience inside a competitive club environment
