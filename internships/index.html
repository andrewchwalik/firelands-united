Internships

Firelands United offers internship opportunities for students and early-career professionals looking for hands-on experience in sports operations.

Internship tracks may include media, match-day operations, communications, analytics, and sponsorship support. Roles and availability can vary by season.

Submit your interest through the contact page and include your area of focus, availability, and any relevant experience.
