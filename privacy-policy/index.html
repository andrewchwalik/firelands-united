Privacy Policy

FUFC operates the firelandsunited.com website, which provides the SERVICE.

Any reference in this Privacy Policy to: “We”, “Us” or “Our” includes Firelands United Football Club (FUFC); “You” or “Your” means You as a User of the Site; and “User(s)” means all and any users or viewers of the Service/Site.

This page is used to inform website visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service, the Firelands United website.

If you choose to use our Service, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation with this policy. The Personal Information that we collect are used for providing and improving the Service. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

The terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms of Service, which is accessible at firelandsunited.com/terms-of-service, unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, phone number, and postal address when using Our Services. By accepting Our Privacy Policy, you consent to Our collection of data described below, to Our use of the data, to the processing of this data, and to Our sharing of the data with third-party processors as needed for Our legitimate business interests.

1.1 Information collection

Personal Data

Personal Data is information that can be used to identify You specifically, including Your name, shipping address, email address, telephone number, or demographic information like Your age, gender, height, weight, shirt size, or hometown. You consent to giving Us this information by providing it to Us voluntarily on Our website or any mobile application.

Derivative Data

Derivative data is information that Our servers automatically collect about You when You access Our Site, such as Your IP address, browser type, the dates and times that You access Our website, and the specific pages You view.

Financial Data

Financial data is data that is related to Your payment method, such as credit card or bank transfer details. We collect financial data in order to allow You to purchase, order, return, or exchange products or services from Our website and any related mobile apps. We store limited financial data. Most financial data is transferred to Our payment processors, PayPal, Apple Pay, or Stripe.

PayPal Privacy Policy: https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/ua/privacy-full

Apple Pay Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/data/en/apple-pay

Stripe Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

Mobile Device Data

If You use Our website via a mobile device or app, We may collect information about Your mobile device, including device ID, model and manufacturer, and location information.

Social Networking Data

We may access personal information from social networking sites and apps, including Facebook, Instagram, Linkedin, Twitter, Snapchat, or other social networking sites or apps not named specifically here.

Other Data

Occasionally, You may give FUFC additional data in order to enter into a contest or giveaway or to participate in a survey.

1.2 How We Use Your Information

Your information allows Us to offer You certain products and services, including the use of Our website, to fulfill Our obligations to You, to customize Your interaction with FUFC and Our Site, and to allow Us to suggest other products and services We think might interest You.

  • Create and administer Your account
  • Deliver products or services purchased by You
  • Correspond with You
  • Process payments or refunds
  • Interact with You via social media
  • Send newsletters and updates
  • Deliver targeted advertising
  • Request feedback and notify You of updates
  • Resolve disputes and troubleshoot issues
  • Administer contests or giveaways
  • Compile anonymous statistical data
  • Assist law enforcement as necessary
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and analyze trends

2. Grounds for Data Use and Processing

FUFC may collect, use, and process Your information based on legitimate business interest, performance of a contract, consent, and as required by law.

3. Reason for Disclosing Information

We may share Your information with third parties in certain situations, including third-party processing, legal obligations, protection of FUFC, business transfers, advertisers, and other general business purposes.

4. Website Analytics

We may partner with third-party analytic companies, including Squarespace Analytics and Google Analytics. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Processing Your Information

For the most part, We do not process Your information in-house, but provide it to third-party processors for processing. However, We may process data internally when needed to administer, maintain, and improve Our services.

5.1 International Data

Our website is hosted by servers located in the United States of America. If You reside outside the United States, some of Your data may be transferred internationally.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as needed for legitimate business purposes, legal obligations, or until You ask Us to delete Your data, subject to applicable legal exceptions.

7. Your Rights

You may request to update account information, confirm personal data use, change consent, request a data copy, transfer data, or delete data.

To update or delete Your information, please contact Us at: hello@firelandsunited.com.

8. Tracking Technologies

We may use log data, cookies, pixel tags, email confirmations, and related technologies to improve Our services and user experience.

9. Service Providers

We currently use GitHub to host our Service. Their Privacy Policy can be accessed here: https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies.

10. Security

We use commercially acceptable means to protect Personal Information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Complaints

If You are an EU resident, You may complain to a supervising authority if You believe We are misusing Your data.

You may also opt out of email communications by contacting: hello@firelandsunited.com.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

You can reach us at: hello@firelandsunited.com
