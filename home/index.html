We Are Firelands United

Firelands United is a minor league soccer club located in the Firelands Region of Northern Ohio. Founded in 2023, our mission is simple: develop players, build community, and grow the beautiful game. We sport both a men's and women's first team in the Northern Ohio Soccer League. Our matches are played throughout Ohio, so join us at our home in Sandusky or on the road!
